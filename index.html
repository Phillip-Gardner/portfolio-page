Dished!

  • With lockdown in full swing, cooking is becoming a chore for young professionals in their late twenties. Our team saw an opportunity for a recipe suggestion app that provided inspiration and excitement to cooking with novel ingredients that you may find at your local market.
  • For our Le Wagon final project, our team of 4 built Dished! over a 2-week product design sprint. We collaborated using Agile methodologies, Git for version control and Heroku for hosting.
  • We focused on developing clean, reusable code and utilised MVC architecture. The backend has 11-models in PostgreSQL - the most complex data schema we had worked with.
  • Our key features include a wish-list that utilises cookies, a Select2 JQuery search and a mosaic style homepage.
  • Product stack: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS/Sass, JavaScript, Ajax, PostgreSQL and ActiveRecord.

Rent-A-Yurt

  • In a 1 week sprint, we designed and built an AirBnB style marketplace that utilised Devise authentication and Mapbox
  • We saw a market niche for yurt rentals in areas of the UK countryside and decided to target festival goers looking for a comfortable place to stay.
  • After designing our wireframe in Figma, we used pair programming to iterate quickly and develop clean code
  • Product stack: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS/Sass, JavaScript, Ajax, PostgreSQL and ActiveRecord
